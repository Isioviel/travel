Day Seventeen: Kyoto

Saturday 20th May 2023

Fushimi Inari & Nara

What We Had Planned

  • Train (Nara Line, 10 minutes) or walk (40 minutes)
  • Spend the morning at Fushimi Inari Taisha, a mountainside shrine, walking up as high as we want to.
  • Snack on fried tofu snacks on the way down.
  • Get back on the train at Inari Station to Nara when we're ready. Takes 45-60 minutes, maybe quicker if we change trains at Rokujizo Station.
  • Walk towards the park, buying a vegetarian curry bento box en route to eat as a picnic.
  • Kōfuku-ji temple and pagoda.
  • Walk past the museum and Nandai-mon gate to reach the main temple, Tōdai-ji, with a massive Buddha statue.
  • Walk back through the Nara Park (looking out for the numerous deer), via Kasuga-taisha shrine.
  • Loop back out of the park past Ukimido pavillion.
  • Head back to Kyoto on the train.
