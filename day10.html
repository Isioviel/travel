Day Ten: Cycling

Saturday 13th May 2023

Kanazawa

What We Had Planned

  • Breakfast at the Ryokan.
  • 18km cycle.
  • Train to Kanazawa.
  • Visit Kenrokuen Garden.
  • We have the afternoon to ourselves, so we can do one or more of the following (we can discuss with the tour guide on the way, and see what will be most unique to Kanazawa):
    • Naga-machi district, including Nomura-ke Samurai Residence
    • Higashi Chaya Geisha district, including Shima feudal-era tea house.
    • Myouryuji Ninja temple with hidden passageways (might need to pre-book a tour).
  • Evening in a modern hotel.
  • We can eat in one of the many restaurants on the lower floors of the hotel, or find somewhere out and about.
