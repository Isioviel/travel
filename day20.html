Day Twenty: Tokyo

Tuesday 23rd May 2023

Shinkansen & Sumo

What We Had Planned

  • Shinkansen back to Tokyo in the morning (takes ~ 2.5hrs, so plan to leave before 10am) - look out for Fuji-san!
  • Leave our bags at Tokyo Station for the day.
  • Travel (includes a change) to Ryōgoku Station.
  • We have Sumo Tournament tickets for the whole day - the main action starts at 2pm.
  • Spend the afternoon in and around Ryogoku Kokugikan National Sumo Stadium, including any of the following:
    • Sumō Museum
    • Edo-Tokyo Museum
    • Japanese Sword Museum
  • Collect our bags and head to our final hotel, near to Hamamatsuchō Station (for easy transit to the airport the next morning).
