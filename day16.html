Day Sixteen: Kyoto

Friday 19th May 2023

East Kyoto

What We Had Planned

  • Train and walk to Higashiyama Jisho-ji, which has a sand mound shaped like Fuji-san!
  • Walk down Philosopher's Path.
  • Slight detour to Okazaki Shrine, another rabbit shrine.
  • Continuing the path, behind Nanzen-ji temple is the Suirokaku Water Bridge aquaduct.
  • Walk along the Keage Incline.
  • Head through Maruyama Park to Yasaka Shrine.
  • Walk through the historic streets of Hanamikoji Street and Shinbashi Dori.
  • Nishiki Market for snacks.
  • Possibly sign up for a Gion (this area) night walking tour.
