Day Three: Tokyo

Saturday 6th May 2023

Imperial Palace & Science

What We Had Planned

Imperial Palace

  • Walk to Tsukiji Outer Market, either via shrines and bridges or directly there depending on what time we get up.
  • Eat random snacks for breakfast and buy tea from Maruyama's Seaweed.
  • Maybe visit Hongwan-ji temple by the market.
  • Walk or metro to the Imperial Palace gardens.
  • Wander the gardens, including the ruins of Edo castle - I downloaded an audio guide tour, so we can take headphones with us and learn some history.
  • Metro back to the apartment for a convenience store lunch and to do laundry, then see how tired we are (expecting jetlag to catch up with us if it hasn't already).
    If we still have energy, proceed to part two, otherwise skip it and relax.

Science & Tech

  • Metro to Odaiba. It will take 40-60 mins and a change or two, there are various route options.
  • See the Unicorn Gundam statue (it moves at 5pm).
  • Various science/tech places, some free, no idea how much time they will take so just do what we fancy:
    • Kawasaki Robostage.
    • National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation
    • Panasonic Center.
  • Walk over the Rainbow Bridge at/after sundown, it lights up.
  • Eat on the other side of the bridge, then metro back.
