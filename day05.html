Day Five: Tokyo

Monday 8th May 2023

Ghibli Museum & Ueno

What We Had Planned

Ghibli Museum

  • Get the train to Mitaka Station - this will take approximately 1h. We have a few options:
    • Get to Tokyo station (walking, or via Hatchōbori Station), then get on the Chuo Line all the way.
    • Get the Tozai Line from Kayabachō Station, then changing to the Chuo Line at Nakano Station.
  • Walk along the Tamagawa Aqueduct to the museum (about 20 minute walk).
  • Ghibli Museum, entrance at 10am.
  • Walk through Inokashira Park.
  • Return from Kichijo-jo Station, it will take approx 1h to get to Ueno.
  • On the way back, maybe jump off the train to visit the Tokyo Metropolitan Building for a free sky view (it is closed when we're nearby the day before).

Ueno

  • If we haven't eaten yet head to Vegan Gyoza Yu for takeaway and picnic in the park.
  • Maybe visit Yanaka Cemetery.
  • Wander Ueno Park, looking at all the museum buildings; they are all closed on Mondays so just admire them from the outside.
  • Meet James & Miki for dinner and drinks in the evening.
  • To return, get the Hibiya Line to Kayabachō Station.
