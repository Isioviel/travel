Day Eighteen: Kyoto

Sunday 21st May 2023

Nara

What We Had Planned

    • Train to Nara (takes about an hour, maybe quicker if we change trains at Rokujizo Station).
    • Walk towards the park, buy food en route to eat as a picnic.
    • Kōfuku-ji temple and pagoda.
    • Walk past the museum and Nandai-mon gate to reach the main temple, Tōdai-ji, with a massive Buddha statue.
    • Long walking trail (example route):
      • Through Nara Park (looking out for the numerous deer).
      • Via Kasuga-taisha shrine.
      • Into the Kasugayama Primeval Forest, past waterfalls and small caves.
      • Loop back out of the park past Ukimido pavillion.
    • Head back to Kyoto on the train.
