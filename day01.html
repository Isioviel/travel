Day One: Tokyo

Thursday 4th May 2023

Arrival & Ginza

What We Had Planned

  • At the airport, get cash and buy IC cards (Suica or Pasmo, whichever we find first!).
  • Monorail & train to Tokyo station - leave our bags in a locker here.
  • Eat vegetarian ramen at T's Tantan (inside the station).
  • Wander to kill time until accommodation check-in, including:
    • See the Ghibli clock at the Nittele Tower if we can time it right (moves at 15:00).
    • Sit and drink tea (I have a list of potential tea cafes!).
    • Buy tea to drink over the next couple of weeks.
    • Get a small notebook for collecting eki stamps at the train stations.
  • Collect Tokyo Station's eki stamp, then check in to our accomodation.
  • Explore the local convenience stores to buy snacks & breakfast food for the next couple of days, and to pick up tickets.
  • Wander the nearby area (via lots of bridges!) to keep awake.
  • Dinner on Tsukishima Monja Street (griddled pancakes).
