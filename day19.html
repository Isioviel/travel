Day Nineteen: Kyoto

Monday 22nd May 2023

Arashiyama

What We Had Planned

  • Train from Kyoto Station to Umahori Station, about 30 minutes.
  • Having pre-booked tickets for the Sagano Scenic Railway earlier in the week, get to Torokko Kameoka station for the first train of the day (9:30am ish).
  • The railway travels down the river. We will get off at the second to last stop, by the Arashiyama Bamboo Forest.
  • Visit Tenryu-ji temple, and eat lunch in their vegetarian restaurant (booked for 12:00).
  • Digest with a hilly 5km walking loop, passing or stopping at:
    • Giouji Temple, with moss garden and bamboo grove.
    • Saga Toriimoto Preserved Street.
    • Adashino Nenbutsuji Temple, with carved stone statues.
    • Mausoleum of Emperor Saga, first emperor to (legendarily) drink tea, this is up some stairs and has a nice view.
    • Daikakuji Temple, has a rock garden.
    • End the loop at the main street, by the Kimono Forest outdoor art exhibit.
  • If we come back from our walk early, walk across the bridge to Arashiyama Monkey Park Iwatayama.
  • Get the San-In line back from Saga-Arashiyama Station.
